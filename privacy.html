Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Bill Simplify is committed to protecting your financial data and personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Apple Sign In Data: Your Apple ID email and display name (if you choose to share it)
  • Account Information: User ID, subscription status, and app preferences
  • Usage Data: App analytics, feature usage, and performance metrics

Bill and Document Data

  • Bill Images: Photos of bills you choose to scan and analyze
  • Analysis Results: AI-generated analysis data, identified issues, and savings calculations
  • Dispute Letters: Generated dispute letter content (if you use this feature)

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Bill Analysis: Process your bill images using AI to identify potential issues and savings
  • Account Management: Manage your subscription, track usage, and provide customer support
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure secure app operation

AI Processing

Your bill images are processed by OpenAI's Vision API to provide analysis results. These images are:

  • Transmitted securely over encrypted connections
  • Processed temporarily for analysis purposes only
  • Not stored permanently by OpenAI
  • Not used for AI training or other purposes

3. Data Storage and Security

Apple CloudKit Storage

  • Your data is stored securely in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • End-to-end encryption protects your information
  • Data is isolated to your Apple ID and not accessible to other users
  • Apple's industry-leading security standards protect your information

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Apple Sign In provides secure, privacy-focused authentication
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Regular Security Reviews: We regularly review and update our security practices

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or bill data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: OpenAI for AI analysis (with strict data protection agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and users
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protections)

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Processes bill images for analysis (no permanent storage)
  • Apple: Handles payments, authentication, and data storage
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage analytics to improve the app

5. Your Privacy Rights

Data Access and Control

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual bills or your entire account
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format (Premium feature)
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. This action:

  • Permanently removes all your bill data from our servers
  • Cancels any active subscriptions
  • Cannot be undone
  • Takes effect immediately

6. Children's Privacy

Bill Simplify is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Users

Bill Simplify is available globally. Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure that all data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes
  • Requiring your consent for material changes that affect your rights

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:
