Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bill Simplify analyze my bills?
Bill Simplify uses advanced AI technology powered by OpenAI's Vision API to analyze your bill images. The AI examines every line item, identifies potential billing errors, duplicate charges, and suspicious fees, then calculates potential savings opportunities.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes, absolutely. We use Apple Sign In for authentication and store your data securely in Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption. Your bill images and analysis results are private and only accessible to you. We never share your financial information with third parties.
What types of bills can I scan?
Bill Simplify can analyze virtually any type of bill including medical bills, utility bills, credit card statements, subscription services, insurance claims, phone bills, and more. Our AI is trained to understand various billing formats and identify potential issues across different industries.
How many bills can I scan with the free version?
The free version allows you to scan 2 bills per month with basic analysis and standard dispute letter generation. Premium subscribers get unlimited scans, advanced AI analysis, fraud detection, PDF export, and priority support.
How accurate are the dispute letters?
Our AI generates professional, legally-sound dispute letters based on the specific issues found in your bills. The letters follow industry best practices and include all necessary details for effective dispute resolution. Premium users get access to enhanced letter templates and customization options.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple App Store account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, and you'll continue to have access to premium features until then.
What should I do if the app can't read my bill?
If the app has trouble reading your bill, try these steps: 1) Ensure good lighting when taking the photo, 2) Hold the camera steady and make sure the bill is flat, 3) Check that all text is clearly visible and not blurry, 4) Try rescanning with better positioning. If issues persist, contact our support team.

Common Troubleshooting Steps

  1. App won't open: Restart your device and try again. Ensure you have the latest version of the app.
  2. Scanning issues: Check camera permissions in Settings > Privacy > Camera > Bill Simplify.
  3. Analysis taking too long: Check your internet connection. Analysis requires a stable connection to our AI servers.
  4. Can't sign in: Ensure Apple Sign In is enabled in Settings > Apple ID > Sign-In & Security.
  5. Subscription issues: Check your App Store account and payment method. Contact Apple Support for billing questions.
  6. App crashes: Force close the app, restart your device, and ensure you have sufficient storage space.
